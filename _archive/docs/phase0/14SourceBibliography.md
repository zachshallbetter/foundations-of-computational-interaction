# Deliverable 14: Source Bibliography

**Program:** Computational Interaction Science (CompInt)  
**Date:** 2026-07-17  
**Status:** Canonical Reference Model  

This document contains the verified master bibliography for the Computational Interaction Science (CompInt) Phase 0 review. It maps foundational papers, empirical studies, and formal treatises to the core concepts they support.

---

## 1. Bibliographic Records

### [Card1983] Card, S. K., Moran, T. P., & Newell, A. (1983)
*   **Title:** The Psychology of Human-Computer Interaction
*   **Publisher:** Lawrence Erlbaum Associates
*   **Year:** 1983
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.interaction_strategy, compint.system_state
*   **Role:** Foundational model of rational human action and cognitive time limits in interfaces.

### [Gibson1979] Gibson, J. J. (1979)
*   **Title:** The Ecological Approach to Visual Perception
*   **Publisher:** Houghton Mifflin
*   **Year:** 1979
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.opportunity_profile, compint.environment
*   **Role:** Foundational theory of physical affordances.

### [Suchman1987] Suchman, L. A. (1987)
*   **Title:** Plans and Situated Actions: The Problem of Human-Machine Communication
*   **Publisher:** Cambridge University Press
*   **Year:** 1987
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.interaction_strategy, compint.interpretation
*   **Role:** Establishes the situated nature of human action in response to local contingencies, refuting static plan execution.

### [Tarski1944] Tarski, A. (1944)
*   **Title:** The Semantic Conception of Truth: and the Foundations of Semantics
*   **Journal:** Philosophy and Phenomenological Research, 4(3), 341-376
*   **DOI:** 10.2307/2102968
*   **Year:** 1944
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.semantic_assignment, compint.semantic_state
*   **Role:** Mathematical foundations of semantic truth conditions.

### [Cousot1977] Cousot, P., & Cousot, R. (1977)
*   **Title:** Abstract Interpretation: A Unified Lattice Model for Static Analysis of Programs
*   **Conference:** Proceedings of the 4th ACM SIGACT-SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages, 238-252
*   **DOI:** 10.1145/512950.512973
*   **Year:** 1977
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.projection
*   **Role:** Formal abstract mappings between logical program states and simplified state spaces.

### [Fitts1954] Fitts, P. M. (1954)
*   **Title:** The Information Capacity of the Human Motor System in Controlling the Amplitude of Movement
*   **Journal:** Journal of Experimental Psychology, 47(6), 381-391
*   **DOI:** 10.1037/h0055392
*   **Year:** 1954
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.opportunity_profile

### [Milner1980] Milner, R. (1980)
*   **Title:** A Calculus of Communicating Systems
*   **Publisher:** Springer-Verlag
*   **Year:** 1980
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.interaction_episode, compint.coupling

*   **Year:** 1978
*   **Concepts Supported:** compint.representation, compint.projection
