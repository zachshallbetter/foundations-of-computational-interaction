# Reference Maintenance Guide

## Authority order

1. Publisher or standards-body record.
2. Crossref or DataCite registry record.
3. National library or ISBN agency record.
4. Conference proceedings record.
5. Author or institutional repository.
6. Secondary bibliographic index.

## Maintenance workflow

- Match by title, author, year, and container; never by title alone.
- Confirm DOI resolution and metadata agreement.
- Confirm the cited edition of books before adding ISBN.
- Preserve separate records for substantively distinct editions.
- Record corrections, retractions, and expressions of concern.
- Do not add ORCID by name similarity alone.
- Re-run duplicate and missing-field reports after every update.
