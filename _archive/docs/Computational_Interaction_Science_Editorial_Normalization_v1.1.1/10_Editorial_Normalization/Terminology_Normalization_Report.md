# Terminology Normalization Report

## Canonical policy

Named contracts are capitalized as canonical artifacts. Canonical concepts retain their established spelling. Generic concepts remain lower case in ordinary prose.

## Applied normalizations

Projection Contract, Interaction Contract, Measurement Contract, Behavioral Evidence Contract, Similarity Contract, Transfer Contract, Strategy Contract, Prediction Contract, and Comparison Contract were normalized where present.

## Prohibited changes

No canonical definition, concept identifier, ontology relation, or formal symbol was changed.
